A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, full name Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam, was an eminent Indian aerospace scientist and statesman who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. Born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a pivotal role in India's civilian space program and military missile development, earning the nickname "Missile Man of India." Known for his humility and inspirational leadership, he authored several books and was a recipient of numerous prestigious awards, including the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor. Abdul Kalam passed away on July 27, 2015, leaving behind a legacy of scientific innovation and public service.



Virat Kohil

Virat KohliVirat Kohli is an Indian cricketer who plays for India national cricket team. He was born in Delhi, India on November 5, 1988. Virat is the first player in ICC cricket history to win all 3 ICC awards in a single year- ICC , ODI player of the year, ICC Test player of the year and ICC Player of the year award in 2018. He is rated as one of the best batsmen in the world. He plays right handed-batting. He is considered as one of the greatest batsmen of all time. In ODIs, he boasts an average of 65.5 runs when batting second and 51.7 runs when batting first.



Prabhas

Prabhas is an Indian actor known for his work primarily in Telugu cinema. His full name is Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju. He gained widespread recognition and popularity for his role as Baahubali in the epic film series "Baahubali: The Beginning" (2015) and "Baahubali: The Conclusion" (2017), directed by S. S. Rajamouli. These films were massive successes and became some of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.



R Praggnanandhaa

Rameshbabu Praggnanandhaa, born on August 10, 2005, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, is a renowned Indian chess prodigy. Known for his exceptional skills and achievements at a young age, Praggnanandhaa became the youngest International Master at the age of 10 years and 10 months in 2016. He further cemented his place in chess history by becoming a Grandmaster at 12 years and 10 months in 2018, making him one of the youngest ever to achieve this title.Praggnanandhaa has competed in numerous high-level tournaments and secured notable victories against top grandmasters.



Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, Gujarat, is the current Prime Minister of India, having held the position since May 26, 2014. A member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS), Modi previously served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. As Prime Minister, he has launched major initiatives such as Make in India, Digital India, and Swachh Bharat Abhiyan. Known for his dynamic leadership and ambitious reforms, Modi aims to drive economic growth and development in India.
